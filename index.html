Music is an art form and cultural activity whose medium is sound. General definitions of music include common elements such as pitch (which governs melody and harmony), rhythm (and its associated concepts of tempo, meter, and articulation).

Music is the language of the soul.

Music is an important part of our lives. It can bring us joy, comfort, and inspiration. Whether you are listening to your favorite song, playing an instrument, or singing along with friends, music has a way of connecting us all.
